About KiNECT

Who Are We?

KiNECT is an online database that helps schools, companies, and individuals find in-person and online community service opportunities. The pandemic made many realize the value of helping others and how hard it can be to find opportunities to give back. So we decided to design a platform that would personalize the community service experience, keeping in mind a user’s location, preferences, and interest throughout the entire process. Our system makes it easier to find events that our users are actually interested in doing. Moreover, Kinect’s signup process easily allows schools to create accounts for their students. With partnerships from a variety of organizations, students can find volunteer work in their area and easily track their hours of community service. What makes us different from other community service locators is the fact that we both simplify the process and make it something that will appeal to individual interests.

Did You Know...

What differentiates us from our main competitor, HelperHelper, KiNECT has personalized volunteer work, and allows students to find and organize volunteer work.
